About Us

Hyperloop UPV is a spanish team of students from the Technical University Valencia designing the Hyperloop, a futuristic train concept that could achieve more than 1000 kph with renewable energies. After taking part in the competition in January 2016 organized by SpaceX (Elon Musk’s company) in Texas, they were awarded "Top Design Concept" and "Best Propulsion Subsystem" among many other teams from most prestigious universities, being one of the only 3 european teams awarded.



SpaceX announced that they will continue celebrating these contests yearly to boost development of the technology, with next big dates being January 2017 and Summmer 2017. After the success in Texas, the Hyperloop UPV team has been invited to participate at the event on January, where they wi'll be demonstrating their "rail-­free" levitation system. Strategically, they have also increased the team size from 5 to more than 30 students. Nevertheless, their objectives are more ambitious:​ to build a full Hyperloop prototype for the competition in California, Summer 2017.
