The idea

Hyperloop is the fifth method of transportation, envisioned by billionaire Elon Musk in 2013: a train that can take you from Los Angeles to San Francisco in 30 minutes at speeds over 900km/h. It consists of a pod that travels inside a low pressure tube while levitating. A powerful compressor at the front of the pod propells it at speeds over the speed of sound. Although it may sound like science fiction, it could be possible sooner than you think.
