The idea

Hyperloop is the fifth method of transportion that billionaire Elon Musk envisioned in 2013. A train that can take you from Los Angeles to San Francisco in 30 minutes at speeds over 900km/h. It consists of a pod that travels inside a low pressure tube while levitating. A powerful compressor at the front of the pod propells the pod at speeds over the speed of sound. Although it sounds like science fiction it could be possible sooner than you think.




Why Choose Us?

Levitation

Our pod design has individual passive magnets paired with electromagnets that are strategically positioned on top of the pod. The pod therefore hangs from the top of a tube, a design that stands out from the competition.

Propulsion

Our propulsion system can be likened to that of an airplane with one key difference. We do not use any fuel. It is 100% renewable and zero emissions.
