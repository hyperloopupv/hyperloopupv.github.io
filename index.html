The idea

Hyperloop is the fifth method of transportion, envisioned by billionaire Elon Musk in 2013: a train that can take you from Los Angeles to San Francisco in 30 minutes at speeds over 900km/h. It consists of a pod that travels inside a low pressure tube while levitating. A powerful compressor at the front of the pod propells the pod at speeds over the speed of sound. Although it may sound like science fiction, it could be possible sooner than you think.

Why Choose our design?

Levitation

Our pod design has individual passive magnets paired with electromagnets that are strategically positioned on top of the pod. This way the pod hangs from the top of the tube, a design that stands out from the competition.

Propulsion

Our propulsion system can be likened to that of an airplane with one key difference: we do not use any fuel. It is 100% renewable and zero emissions.
