Propulsion

Our propulsion system can be likened to that of an airplane with one key difference, we do not use any fuel. It is 100% renewable and zero emissions. It heavily depends on the use of rechargeable batteries as its main source of power. The pod has a front mounted compressor which scoops all the air it encounters and redirects it behind the capsule. Before the air exits the back of our pod it is expanded with a nozzle. This expansion process creates the thrust we need as well as allowing the pod to move through the tube with little to no friction at high speeds, where air density causes an increase in friction.

Levitation

Our pod design has individual passive magnets paired with electromagnets that are strategically positioned on top of the pod. These electromagnets allow individual fine control of the repulsion and attraction of the magnets. The pod therefore hangs from the top of tube without the use of any external aids. Other systems rely on existent Maglev technoology. It consists of levitating on top of a rail. Apart from requiring magnets along the entire length of the pod, the rail increases the cost of the infrastructure by an order of magnitude.
